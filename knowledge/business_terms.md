# Pharmaceutical Business Definitions

- **High Value Doctor (HVD)**: A doctor who has been visited more than 3 times in a month OR has a category 'A'.
- **Low Performing Area**: Any area where the sum of sales targets is less than the average sales target of all areas.
- **Active Manager**: A manager who has at least 5 planned visits in the current `doctor_plan` table.
- **Market Share**: The ratio of internal product sales to IMS (IQVIA) market sales in the same brick.
- **ROI Calculation**: ROI = (Sales Generated - Investment Amount) / Investment Amount.
- **Internal Sales (Company Sales)**: Actual sales generated by the company's own field team, primarily stored in `master_sale` or `invoice_details` (tracked via `product_quantity` and `total_amount`). Always preferred over the empty `orders` table.
- **Market Sales (IMS Data)**: Total sales of a product category in a specific geography by ALL companies in the market, stored in the external `ims_sale` table (tracked via `unit`).
- **Brick (IMS Brick)**: The specific geographical zone or boundary used by external data providers (IMS/IQVIA) to report 'Market Sales'. Company territories are often mapped to these external bricks.
- **Calls / Visits**: In the pharmaceutical industry, a "Call" is another term for a "Visit" to a doctor. These are always recorded and counted in the `doctor_plan` table. Do NOT hallucinate tables like `doctor_calls` or `visits`.
- **Geography Acronyms (RM, ZM, TM)**: The database uses specific acronyms for geographical divisions:
  - **RM (Region)**: Stored in the `regions` table (Example name: `RM - KHI - 1`)
  - **ZM (Zone)**: Stored in the `zones` table (Example name: `ZM - KHI - 1`)
  - **TM (Territory/Area)**: Stored in the `areas` table (Example name: `TM - KHI - 1`)
  Always use `ILIKE` when searching these acronyms as users may not type exact dashes.
- **Sales Database Mapping**:
  - **Doctor Sales**: In `master_sale`, doctors are recorded as `customer_name`. ALWAYS filter by `customer_type = 'Doctors'`. The column `doctor_name` does NOT exist.
  - **Product Groups**: Use `product_group_name`. Note: In current mock data, only `Product Group 1` exists.
  - **Karachi Mapping**: Karachi area is labeled as `TM - KHI` (e.g., `TM - KHI - 1`). Use `ILIKE '%KHI%'` when searching for Karachi.
  - **Manager Filtering**: Use `area_manager_name` or `territory_manager_name` directly in `master_sale`.
  - **Dates**: Always use `invoice_date` (NOT `sale_date`). Use `month_number` (1-12) or `year_number` (e.g., 2024, 2025).
