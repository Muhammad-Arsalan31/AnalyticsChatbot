# Pharmaceutical Business Definitions

- **High Value Doctor (HVD)**: A doctor who has been visited more than 3 times in a month OR has a category 'A'.
- **Low Performing Area**: Any area where the sum of sales targets is less than the average sales target of all areas.
- **Active Manager**: A manager who has at least 5 planned visits in the current `doctor_plan` table.
- **Market Share**: The ratio of internal product sales to IMS (IQVIA) market sales in the same brick.
- **ROI Calculation**: ROI = (Sales Generated - Investment Amount) / Investment Amount.
